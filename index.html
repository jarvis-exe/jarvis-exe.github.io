Privacy Policy for Jarvis.exe Chrome Extension

Introduction

Jarvis.exe is a Chrome extension designed to simplify data entry and data visualization for its users. This privacy policy outlines how we handle, use, and protect your data in relation to the permissions our extension requires: "identity", "identity.email", "storage", and "activeTab".

Information Collection and Use

Permissions Explained:

  • Identity & Identity.Email: We use these permissions to authenticate you and access your Google account email address. This information is crucial for personalizing your experience and ensuring secure access to our services.
  • Storage: This permission allows Jarvis.exe to store data locally on your device. We store settings, preferences, and any data related to the extension's functionality to enhance your user experience and ensure the extension operates efficiently.
  • ActiveTab: We request this permission to interact with the web page content in the active tab when you initiate our extension. This is used solely for the purpose of data entry and visualization within the context of the web page you are viewing.

How We Use Collected Information:

To Provide and Enhance Our Services: The information and permissions are critical for the extension to function as intended, offering personalized and efficient service.

For Authentication and Security: Your email and identity are used to securely authenticate your access, ensuring that your data and settings are safely managed.

Data Storage: Information stored locally on your device is used to remember your settings and preferences, facilitating a tailored user experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy:

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).
  • Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), to provide the Service on our behalf, or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100%. Only Octopus energy has access to the data.

Data Retention

Jarvis.exe retains your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations (for example, if we are required to retain your data to comply with applicable laws), resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain data protection rights. Jarvis.exe aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data. If you wish to be informed about what Personal Data we hold about you and if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

  • Right to Access, Update or Delete the Information We Have on You: Whenever made possible, you can access, update, or request deletion of your Personal Data directly within your account settings section. If you are unable to perform these actions yourself, please contact us to assist you.
  • Right of Rectification: You have the right to have your information rectified if that information is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to our processing of your Personal Data.
  • Right of Restriction: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to be provided with a copy of the information we have on you in a structured, machine-readable, and commonly used format.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
